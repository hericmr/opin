Statement of Solidarity and Peace

At Native Land Digital, we stand in solidarity with all Indigenous peoples and communities around the world who struggle for self-determination, dignity, and the right to live freely on their lands. We acknowledge the deep histories, trauma, and resilience held within Palestine and the surrounding region. As an Indigenous-led organisation, our commitment is to uphold the values of relationality, respect, and peace. We reject all forms of extremism, violence, and hate - wherever they arise.

Our work is grounded in the belief that all peoples deserve to live safely, with their cultures, lands, and futures protected. We seek to share stories and voices that nurture understanding, healing, and peace - honouring the humanity and interconnectedness of us all.
